About Guber

Guber is an online web app that optimizes transportation. It sends a message to the main driver: Mark Getshman, in order to make a pickup to whichever destination. There can also be no destination, for entartainment purposes only.

The Mermaz - A Quick History

July 1916 - An infamous Palestinian bafoon named "Ilan" establishes a social-network called "The Center" at a local fishing hub.

August 1916 - An dark skinned Iraqi settler named "Mazan" takes ownership of "The Center" and publicly renames it "The Merzer"

Second-Half of August 1916 - A Polish usurper infiltrates "The Merzer" ranks and gains favourable admiration.

September 1917 - "The Merzer" loses credentiality. A few accelerationists continue operating the movement conspicuously.
